Perception of self.

A trail of destruction paved with good intentions. How ironic that that is something I always felt described me and my past, present and general life. How fucking sad that I only just realized, at 25 years old, after years and years of writing, speaking that phrase. Only now have I really grasped the start of that sentance. I have always focused on the intentions of my actions. I have always had an optimistic outlook on life that I failed to read, literally just switched off completely to the beginning of that sentence altogether, fuck, I used it only the other day to describe myself when I was asked too, in a few words or less. I hung onto the last few words ever so casually……..



I guess I wrote that sometime in 2016.
