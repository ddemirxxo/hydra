i have the clumsiest soul you will ever meet.

yesterday i tripped over my own confidence,

landed on my pride

and it shattered like the glass of a brand new iphone screen.

this stumble shook my senses loose

and unhinged all my gratitude,

mixing up my pleasantries with my discernings

so every time i shake my head

in disagreement to a compliment,

i feel the knocking

and rattling

of “thankyous” and “go fuck yourselves”.

i’ve been told i have cold hands

because my heart sends frost bite

through my veins

all the way to my finger nails

and to the tips of my toes.

the colour of your heart? red

the colour of mine? eight different shades

of blues and purples

from beating myself up over things i cannot fix.

do you ever wonder what your bed sheets

say about you

when you’re not around?

i wonder what the front of my bedroom door

would do

if it found out about all the things

i’ve done behind it’s back.

or what my cups of tea would think

if my lips poured secrets back into them

after every sip.

i have a dirty clothes basket

full of really, really, really loud mistakes

and a graveyard in my closet.
