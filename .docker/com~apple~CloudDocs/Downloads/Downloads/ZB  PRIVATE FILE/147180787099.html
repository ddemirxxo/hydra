Addiction

It’s funny that now I’ve been threw so many withdrawal symptoms of so many different narcotics.

I could copy and paste the way I have felt when I’ve been hurt, lied to or deceived and the description words would match perfectly, I hadn’t consciously gone threw withdrawals before I had written them and now I read over the things I wrote the first time you broke my heart and I am still trying to understand why that hurt, why the exact way my skin crawled, my muscles twitched and my body shuttered is word for word the way withdrawing feels.

Is love really like a drug or is drugs really like love?

Does it matter, they should both be illegal.

I would have been caught the minute i met you from the profound amounts of love I instantly felt and I would be doing three life sentences in a high max prison as we speak.

Instead of a paper trail the police would just follow the bright red trail of my self destruction or just get a guilt detector and watch the radar direct them straight to my heart.

When my heart broke the first time, I guess it would be like having a drug addiction taken not just out of your reach but pulled and sucked directly out of your blood stream.

You hardly have time to react before the withdrawals set in, the shakes cloud your mind while the emptiness drips out what’s left of my rational thought. Straight out of the gaping holes in my arms and neck.

It’s weird.

I guess weening off love wouldn’t be ideal, although weening off drugs wouldn’t be much better.
