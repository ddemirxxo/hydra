Every rose has its thorns

I’ve always liked that quote.

Although I’m not that into roses and I wouldn’t use ‘thorn’ as a descriptive word for your negative traits.

You are ten times more beautiful than all the roses in the world and a sledge hammer sprinkled with rusty needles and blunt rocks to my scull would best describe the pain that’s come with the beauty of you.

If I had a word that would do you justice in the sense of admiration and impeccable looks and sexual appeal the sledge hammer wouldn’t seem so drastic.

I would take the sledge hammer on a daily basses if it meant I could call you mine each of those days.
