Chloe...

Its weird you had to ask for me write about you.

You may not realise this but my days, all my decisions and a mojority of my actions are all based on, around and because of you.

Yes, good and bad.

You are like a cut tendon in the finger I use most to type. It’s not crippling, it’s impossible to ignore. It’s the reason i write the way i do but it’s frustrating as hell. It’s a constant but only affects me when i try and shake it, it shoots pain threw my whole being but it does it in that sensuous addictive way. I only ever get a glimpse of the scar but everyone else notices it before anything.

You weren’t worried about what i would write huh? You were more worried about me just writing about you in general.

I write to escape you.

Not in the way you first grasped, no.
But it is an escape for our fate, our hope and our possible.

You spend your days pulling at short straws and empty promises while i fill my days with fear and and inconsistencies that result in my empty promises faling back on my mouth like barb wire sewn threw my gums.

I never make sense when it comes to you do i?
