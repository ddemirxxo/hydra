Silence

my heart has its own beat
it has its own rhythm
on a scale of 1-5 for originality
it’d be about a 4
you know sometimes when you listen to a song
and it sounds similar to another
so you play them side by side
for comparison
but in comparison
the beats are off
the rhythm is out of whack
and the song lyrics have nothing in common?
thats how i would describe my past lovers
our hearts sounded similar
but side by side
they were never melodic with one another
then there’s you
i feel like if we ever crossed paths in person
as total strangers
we’d both have headphones in
music blaring
with exactly the same song
at exactly the same time
at exactly the same speed
but be none the wiser
i believe that by being in your actual presence
our hearts would beat in sync
at exactly the same time
at exactly the same speed
at exactly the same lyric
at exactly the same volume
i just fear that once i left your presence
the absence of your synchronised heart
would interfere with the audio of mine

have you ever played a song
with just one speaker?
sounds like absolute shit, doesn’t it?
