Like a kid again

I have more fault lines than the colouring books my 6 yr old has scribbled over.
The only thing about me that’s straight to the point is my spine.
and even that’s something my 6 yr old still has more of than I do.
I’m sorry for the abrupt goodbye.
but it had to be done.
If you and someone have written more poems about how sad they make each other
then it has to be done.
If you have written more goodbye messages than, “good morning, beautiful”
then it has to be done.
We are so back and forth from “i miss you” to “fuck you”
that I’m starting to get sea sick.
I’ve been on 8 boats in my entire life.
not one has made me as nauseas
as our unsteady waves of uncertainty.
I’m sorry I’ve become that sore thought
that you have to wrestle with each and every night
I’m sorry that I’m all words, no actions.
but please don’t think that my leaving you
is something that I want
maybe I’m being selfish
maybe I’m being inconsiderate
but I cannot bare another day
of hearing how I went from the girl who filled your stomach with butterflies
to the girl who fills your heart with sorrow
You weren’t something I was ready for
You completely side swiped me
that if we were an actual car crash
i’d still be in the shop, having my damages be assessed.
I didn’t plan on meeting anyone
and I’m sure you didn’t either
I’m sorry for meeting you
because all I’ve done is made you a coffee filled with broken promises
with a teaspoon of I’m sorrys
when all you asked for was two sugars
but I’m not sorry for meeting you
you’ve made me experience a feeling that I’ll never understand
that I’ll never want to
but will spend the rest of my life trying TO understand

I swore I’d never get in over my head

but the day I met you
I was already up to my ankles
