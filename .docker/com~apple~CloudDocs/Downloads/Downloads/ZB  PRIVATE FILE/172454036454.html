Headings.

Those dam bold letters at the top of a page you are about to fill with exhausted attempts of expressing the way you feel.

Headings are my enemy.

Why?

You should know, it is a little unconsious battle everyone has every day.

We have this urge as humans too make sure what we show the world and other people is exactly the way we want, is suitable, intelligently and or perceived correctly when it comes too a majority of our interactions in daily life…..


The only thing is, my ability to show and express emotions of any level in any circumstances is my biggest and most exhausting battle of my life today.

So when something as simple as a heading, a bold and usually short script at the top of the page, simply giving you insight or a quick overview of what your about to read may be easy enough, might even help most people stay on topic throughout whatever they might have to write, well

Fuck those guys.
