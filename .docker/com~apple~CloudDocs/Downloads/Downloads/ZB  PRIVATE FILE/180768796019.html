Imagine if every lie you ever told was written across the sky? Imagine if every promise you ever broke got lodged in your throat? Imagine if each regret you ever had was smeared on your skin for the world to see??

Guess it wouldn’t matter what you imagined, we will never experience any of these things. 

We are masters of deception and even more so in creating distractions.

What matters is that I would write down every lie I have ever told, I would spit out every broken promise and tattoo every regret I ever experienced onto my body for you to see. I know vulnerability is a necessity for people to gain connection and insight, I just don’t understand why vulnerability makes me crazy.

Maybe it’s because im crazy about you

Or maybe it’s because ive never been vulnerable for anyone else….
