BPD

I am never sure of anything

But I will always be the one who speaks with absolute certainty.

I have the worst memory

But I remember everyone who has ever hurt me and how.

I am 110% insecure at all times

Yet I portray enough confidence to fool almost everyone i meet.

I am incapable of feeling guilt

Yet I spend majority of my days drowning in shame.

I am constantly lost in my own thoughts

Still waiting for one of those thoughts to be rational or realistic.

I can read a person in a matter of minutes

So I can make sure you never pay too close attention to me, ever.

Anger is my secondary emotion

My primary is usually impossible for me to identify anyway.

I am loyal to the core

Yet i have cheated on every girl ive ever dated.

I have strong values and beliefs

Everything ive ever done goes completely against all of them.

I can love with my entire being

But i will not feel a thing and can even forget you within 24 hours.

I will tell you exactly what you want to hear

Even if It is a blatant lie.

I will always always be late

Because i made ten plans with ten people and ive spent all my time either avoiding their calls all together or lie about why i cant come because im petrified of losing a friend.
