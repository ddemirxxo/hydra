Destructive

How can I deny this word isn’t the first to cross many minds when asked to describe me?

It’s exactly what I am.

It’s definitely what I do.

It is ingrained inside me, circulating in my blood stream for so long it’s now coating every vein in my body.

My bones are hollow and my body is struggling to get enough blood to my heart.

My hands shake and my chest is always heavy.

My skin is stretched and aching to split open and release the destruction that has made its home inside my body.
